# Verdant Defenders - How to Play

## Overview
Verdant Defenders is a roguelike deckbuilder where you control a nature mage exploring dangerous dungeons. Build your deck, defeat enemies, and survive as long as possible!

## Gameplay Basics

### Combat System
- **Energy**: You get 3 energy per turn to play cards
- **Cards**: Your deck contains Strike (damage), Tactic (utility), and Ritual (ongoing effects) cards
- **Health**: Your life force - when it reaches 0, the game restarts
- **Block**: Temporary armor that absorbs damage until your next turn

### Card Types
- **Strike Cards** (Red tint): Deal damage to enemies
  - Thorn Lash: 6 damage, +2 if target has Seeds
  - Vine Whip: Hits twice for 4 damage each
  - Petal Spray: Hits all enemies for 3 damage
  
- **Tactic Cards** (Green tint): Provide utility and defense
  - Seed Shield: Gain 6 Block and plant a Seed
  - Sprout Heal: Heal 5 HP and draw a card
  - Thorn Wall: Gain 10 Block and damage attackers
  
- **Ritual Cards** (Blue tint): Powerful ongoing effects
  - Growth Ritual: Gain energy bonuses
  - Garden Bloom: Draw cards and gain Seeds at end of turn
  - Growth Aura: All Strikes deal +2 damage

### Progression
- **Rooms**: Fight through 20 rooms to win
- **Boss Fights**: Every 5th room is a boss battle with special abilities
- **Rewards**: Gain gold and healing for clearing rooms
- **Scaling**: Enemies get stronger as you progress

### Boss Battles
- **Phases**: Bosses have multiple phases with different abilities
- **Special Attacks**: Each boss has unique devastating abilities
- **Rewards**: Defeating bosses gives bonus gold and healing

## Tips for Success
1. **Balance your deck**: Use a mix of damage, defense, and utility
2. **Manage your energy**: Don't waste energy - plan your turns
3. **Watch enemy intents**: Prepare defenses when enemies plan big attacks
4. **Use Block wisely**: Block cards can save your life against strong attacks
5. **Boss preparation**: Save powerful cards and healing for boss fights

## Current Features
- Full turn-based card combat system
- 60+ unique cards with various effects
- Enemy AI with intent system
- Boss battles with phases and special abilities
- Progressive difficulty scaling
- Block/damage combat mechanics
- Gold and reward systems
- Automatic game restart on victory/defeat

## Controls
- **Click cards** to play them
- **End Turn button** to finish your turn
- **Mouse hover** over cards for visual feedback

Enjoy defending the verdant realm!
